Privacy Policy

What this site collects

This website does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. No personal data is collected automatically when you visit.

The contact page uses a mailto link to open your email client. Any information you include in that email (your name, email address, and message) is sent directly to gce@gunnercastle.com and is not stored on this website or any third-party server.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (such as your IP address) as part of their infrastructure. You can review GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Third-party services
